Eco-festival "Pasternak"
November 17, 2019
On November 17th, Cotton Hall will host the cozy Pasternak Autumn Festival. Again, in a pleasant atmosphere, old and new friends of the festival will gather. They will gather to discuss their environmental habits, pump their skills into “zero waste”, eat tasty and varied vegan cuisine, cook interesting dishes with famous people at cooking classes and just spend time with people who are close to them.
Green cities of the future: from Oslo to Gomel
November 27, 2019
On November 27, 2019 in Gomel, for the first time as part of the Nordic Talks project, an international expert, Motren Nordskag (Norway), will perform his research and professional activities aimed at developing political and economic measures to protect the environment.
Zero weste-it's not just a rubbish
November 19, 2019
The environmental solutions center ecoidea.by on November 19 invites you to a lecture “Zero waste - is not only about rubbish”, which will be devoted to the “zero” philosophy of consumption.
City environmental action "Day without a car"
September 22, 2019
On September 22, from 14.00 to 21.00 on Oktyabrskaya St., an open-air holiday will be held dedicated to the Day without a car. Free admission. The main condition is without cars! Come on a scooter, bike, hoverboard or come on foot.
